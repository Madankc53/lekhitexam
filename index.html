Tips to Pass the Likhit Exam

  • Focus heavily on traffic signs — most candidates lose marks here.
  • Practice with timed mock tests so 30 minutes feels comfortable, not rushed.
  • Review wrong answers immediately — don't just retake blindly.
  • Know category-specific rules (helmet law for Cat A, seatbelt law for Cat B).
  • Read every option fully before choosing — some questions test exceptions.

Know Your Traffic Sign Categories

Every sign in Nepal falls into one of these types — recognizing the shape and color tells you the meaning instantly.

🚫

Prohibition Signs

Red circle — an action is not allowed.

⚠️

Warning Signs

Yellow/red triangle — hazard ahead.

➡️

Mandatory Signs

Blue circle — instruction must be followed.

ℹ️

Information Signs

Blue/green square — services, directions.
