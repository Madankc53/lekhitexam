About Likhit Exam Nepal

Likhit Exam Nepal is a free, independent practice platform built to help candidates prepare for Nepal's driving license written (Likhit) exam. We provide timed mock tests, traffic sign practice, and exam information covering Category A (motorcycle/scooter) and Category B (car/jeep/van) license categories.

This platform is part of the thenepal.io network, which also runs educational and utility tools for Nepal including GPA-to-percentage conversion, Preeti-to-Unicode font conversion, and national symbol reference sites.

We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to the Department of Transport Management (DoTM) or any government body of Nepal. Our content is created and maintained independently to support self-study.
